            <section class="ingred_proced">
                <div class="ingredients">
                    <h2 class="ing_proc_h2">Ingredients</h2>
                    <ul class="list-ingredients">
                        <li>1 Piece Whole Chicken</li>
                        <li>2 Sachets MAGGI® Magic Sarap®</li>
                        <li>0.25 tsp Ground Pepper</li>
                        <li>2 tbls Maggi® Soy Cooking And Seasoning Sauce</li>
                        <li>1 tbl Brown Sugar</li>
                        <li>1 Head Garlic</li>
                        <li>2 Stalks Lemongrass</li>
                    </ul>
                </div>
                <div class="procedure">
                    <h2 class="ing_proc_h2">Procedure</h2>
                    <div class="steps">
                        <h4>Step 1</h4>
                        <p>Marinate chicken with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®, pepper, soy souce, sugar,
                            garlic and lemongrass overnight in the refrigerator.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="steps">
                        <h4>Step 2</h4>
                        <p>Remove chicken from the refrigerator 1 hour before cooking. Truss with butcher’s twine
                            and transfer into a roasting pan.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="steps">
                        <h4>Step 3</h4>
                        <p>Roast in a 350°F preheated oven for 1 hour 15 minutes. Remove from the oven and
                            set aside to rest. Cut into serving pieces and baste with the drippings. Serve immediately.
                        </p>
                    </div>
                </div>
