Lechon Manok

Lechon manok is a Filipino spit-roasted chicken dish made with chicken marinated in a mixture of garlic,bay leaf, onion, black pepper, soy sauce, and patis (fish sauce). The marinade may also be sweetened with muscovado or brown sugar.

Prep Time

20 mins.

Cook Time

30 mins.

Servings

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Piece Whole Chicken
  • 2 Sachets MAGGI® Magic Sarap®
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 2 tbls Maggi® Soy Cooking And Seasoning Sauce
  • 1 tbl Brown Sugar
  • 1 Head Garlic
  • 2 Stalks Lemongrass

Procedure

Step 1

Marinate chicken with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®, pepper, soy souce, sugar, garlic and lemongrass overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2

Remove chicken from the refrigerator 1 hour before cooking. Truss with butcher’s twine and transfer into a roasting pan.

Step 3

Roast in a 350°F preheated oven for 1 hour 15 minutes. Remove from the oven and set aside to rest. Cut into serving pieces and baste with the drippings. Serve immediately.
